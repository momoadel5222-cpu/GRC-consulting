1

First Line of Defense

Operations & Management
Business units and operational management own and manage risks and controls on a day-to-day basis. They are responsible for implementing corrective actions to address process and control deficiencies.

Key Responsibilities:

Execute daily business operations
Implement risk management policies
Maintain effective internal controls
Conduct customer due diligence
Report suspicious activities
Execute transaction monitoring
Train frontline staff
Document procedures and controls
2

Second Line of Defense

Risk Management & Compliance
Compliance and risk management functions provide oversight, monitoring, and guidance. They establish frameworks, policies, and procedures while remaining independent from the first line.

Key Responsibilities:

Develop AML/CFT policies and procedures
Conduct risk assessments
Monitor regulatory changes
Provide compliance guidance
Review suspicious activity reports
Conduct compliance testing
Report to senior management
Coordinate training programs
3

Third Line of Defense

Internal Audit
Internal audit provides independent assurance to the board and senior management on the effectiveness of governance, risk management, and internal controls across both first and second lines.

Key Responsibilities:

Conduct independent audits
Assess control effectiveness
Evaluate compliance program
Review risk management processes
Test AML/CFT controls
Report audit findings to board
Follow up on remediation
Provide assurance on governance
